Who is Hayden Platt?

A Game Designer

Games for me create experiences and emotions unlike any other medium. This passion paired with my love for computers has led me to a career in video game design. Currently leading a student project, it's my personal goal to create a game that exceeds the trends and formulas that are appearing in the industry. I'm in it to make genuinely innovative, engaging experiences with great people.

A Leader

I've been naturally drawn to leadership for as long as I can remember. Over the years I've led many small teams, but this summer I wanted to really challenge myself. I chose to put internship hunting on hold for a year, and lead a project of my own. 8 months later, Ursa Major Games released an Alpha I'm very proud of. Not a day goes by where I don't love what we're doing.

A Student

While true that I'm a student at UC Santa Cruz, that's not the only place I do my learning. When I play games, I'm learning. When I lead projects, I'm learning. I've come to realize that I'll never know everything about games, but that's also what makes creating them so great. While my title may change, I'll always be a student with more to learn.
